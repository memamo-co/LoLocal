Benefits

How will my contribution benefit me?

Contribution has no side effect. You can get multiple benefits of contributing your skills / project to "LoLocal". This will improve your chances of achieving your goals.

Exchange Of Thoughts

We all require creative ideas to advance our work, but often run out of ideas. By uploading your project to "LoLocal," individuals can remark on one another's projects and provide ideas and tips for how to make their own projects better.

Creative Projects

Find helpful projects quickly on "LoLocal." Take inspiration from others' creations and add your own to help others in learning. Project-based learning helps students develop coding skills and problem-solving skills.

Engagement & Productivity

Continuous engagement will help you find specific and measurable goals for upcoming projects. You will be able to focus on your goal or improve project planning. Appreciation from people will motivate you to maintain and improve your excellent performance.

Greater Reach

Get more viewers or contributions. The ability to identify, articulate and share a clear vision for the project is one of the key ingredients to project success. Collaboration isn't just a good thing for you, but is essential for the ultimate success of the project.
